Product Description

Our Premium Wireless Headphones with Noise Cancellation redefine your listening experience. Engineered with advanced acoustic technology, these headphones deliver studio-quality sound with deep, accurate bass response and crystal-clear highs.

The active noise cancellation feature uses microphones to analyze ambient sound and generates an equal but opposite signal to cancel out unwanted noise, allowing you to focus on your music or calls.

Features include:

  • Advanced noise cancellation technology
  • 30-hour battery life with quick charge (5 mins = 4 hours)
  • Premium comfort with memory foam ear cushions
  • Built-in microphone for hands-free calls
  • Bluetooth 5.0 with 30ft wireless range
  • Foldable design with carrying case included
